Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. Trinity is a private, liberal arts and sciences institution and was founded in 1869. The student body consists of approximately 2,300 undergraduate and 200 graduate students.


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

 

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are how the class size is small so the teachers are able to get to know the students and work with them. They also focus a lot on the student’s success at the school along with their future. The school has some top quality athletics for a D3 school and take pride in their programs.


I chose Trinity University for many reasons. I wanted to stay in Texas and continue playing soccer. Along with the sports and the location, I knew the academics where top class and that I could push myself academically wise here.
